Trackbox, the Simple and Beautiful Music Player

Experience your music collection, built around you

Simple, beautiful interface for your whole music library

Clean, focused, and productive interface designed from the ground up to help you organize and play your songs with ease. No clutter or gimmicky features; it's just you and your music.


Tags & Pages: Sort and play your music with ease

Playlists are inefficient. They're slow, static, stupid, and single-use. They limit you to adding only individual songs. We set out to fix that with Pages. In Trackbox, add anything you want to a Page, and the songs carry along with it. Add any combination of artists, albums, search terms, ratings, tags, and more. As your library grows, fitting songs are added to your Pages.

Smarter playback for a smarter world

Shuffle is stupid. Random isn't random. In Trackbox, build a mix in a matter of seconds with a playback filter. Enjoy hours of your favorite music selected for you without low rated songs or common repeats. Love your mix? Save it for later or pin it as a Page.

100% open to customization, plug-ins, and skins

Entirely free and open source
